Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and fry the pancetta with garlic until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs, grated parmesan, and black pepper. Set aside.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, but keep some pasta water aside.
  5. Add the spaghetti to the pancetta and garlic, then remove the pan from heat.
  6. Stir in the egg mixture and a splash of pasta water, mixing well to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve hot, topped with extra parmesan and black pepper if desired.

Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Carbonara!
